Mercy Street

Volunteer
 

Mercy Street partners with Dallas churches to match Christian mentors with 4th-12th grade children in West Dallas public schools. The mentor is tasked with being an encourager, friend, and advisor to the child over many years. Through the mutually transforming process, future leaders for the Kingdom are born. Will you join the movement?
